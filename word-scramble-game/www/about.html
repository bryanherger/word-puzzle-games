About Word Scramble

Word Scramble is a fun and educational game designed to challenge your word-building skills. Created with love for my daughter, this game uses a set of letter tiles to form multiple words of varying lengths.

Features

  • Drag-and-drop letter tiles to form words.
  • Unique puzzles with 5 or 6 letters, randomly selected each time you reset.
  • Touch and mouse support for playing on any device.
  • Help dialog to guide new players.
